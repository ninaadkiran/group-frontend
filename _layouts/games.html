STEER CLEAR

Galaxy Game is an expanded version of the 1962 Spacewar!, potentially the first video game to spread to multiple computer installations. It features a spaceship, "the needle", maneuvering in the gravity well of a star. The ship has one goal: don't hit the asteroid.

DODGE THE ASTROIDS

Dodge the Asteroids is a spinoff of the original and beloved game flappybird, but uses space and flight to dodge asteroids instead of pipes

ESCAPE THE ASTEROID BELT

Escape the Asteroid Belt is a simple game that revolves around the idea of a lost astronaut in space trying to reach home, navigate your way through a maze of walls and reach your base!
